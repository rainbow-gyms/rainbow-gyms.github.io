# About the Rainbow Gyms App #
## Why I Decided to Create This App ##
Rainbow Gyms is an application that makes balancing the gym and schoolwork for computer engineering, which is my major, and computer science students alike with beginner to intermediate fitness levels. Due to a surge in global interests of going to the gym in the last decades, having physique goals does not only mean becoming stronger but also becoming more confident and ready-to-tackle fearful challenges head on. Although our majors mean that we are constantly in front of papers or computers thinking about logic and maths, going to the gym without a buddy seems daunting so we become unmotivated. This repeated pattern can mean weeks, if not months, of wasted opportunity that could've been spent building your muscle base and getting to know the reins of gym equipments. 

## Goal of This App ##
After getting familiar with this app, the user can expect to have progressed in their muscle-building journey whether that is being able to hold heavier weights or simply meeting new friends. The first time a person downloads the app, he/she will most likely try to search for people who seem trustworthy to go with at first. This is where the "level" system comes in. The more a person presses "join" or "start a gym session", then the more points he/she is able gather up. This means better looking profile borders and unlock features that new people wouldn't have. 

This app uses a calendar that a user can tap in order to start a gym session schedule for others to join.
